Hi there! I am Dr. Frankline Ambetsa.

Data holds no secrets for me, as I navigate through the intricacies of data science and analytics using tools such as IBM SPSS, RStudio, SAS, Excel, Python, and Power BI. My academic journey has also seen me delve into the world of academic writing, crafting research papers, dissertations, and essays with finesse, particularly in STEM subjects.
